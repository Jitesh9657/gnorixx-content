## Introduction
Writing great scripts doesn't have to take hours. When you understand the basic psychology of retention, you can hook your audience in the first 3 seconds...

### The 3-Second Hook Rule
Your hook needs to address a specific pain point immediately. Here is why most creators fail:

* They introduce themselves first (nobody cares yet).
* They ramble about the background.
* They don't promise a clear takeaway.

Instead, start with a bold statement or a question.
